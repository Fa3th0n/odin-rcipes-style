Greek (Choriatiki) Salad

Description

This Greek salad recipe is incredibly good!

It's nice and tangy and even better in the summer when you use fresh vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 2 large tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 10 olives
  • Feta cheese
  • 6 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and peper to taste

Steps

  • Combine tomatoes, cucumber, olives, bell peppers, and red onion in a large bowl.
  • Whisk olive oil, vinegar, oregano, salt and pepper together in a small bowl.
  • Pour dressing over salad, toss well to combine.
  • Place feta cheese on top and enjoy.
